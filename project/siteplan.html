PlayStation Excellence

Site Name

PlayStation Excellence — This name highlights Sony’s long-standing commitment to quality gaming experiences. It focuses on the excellence and innovation that has defined each PlayStation console generation.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to explore the history of PlayStation through its iconic games, design evolution, and impact on gaming culture. Visitors will learn about Sony’s philosophy of innovation, creativity, and storytelling through interactive sections and visuals.

Scenarios

  • What factors have made Sony so successful with the PlayStation brand?
  • Which exclusive studios and games define PlayStation’s success?
  • How has the PlayStation brand evolved across different console generations?

Color Schema

Primary Color: PlayStation Blue (#003791) – used for headers, accents, and branding elements.

Secondary Color: Light Gray (#f4f4f4) – used for backgrounds to maintain contrast.

Accent Color: White (#ffffff) – for text backgrounds and contrast against the Sony blue headings.

Typography

  • Orbitron: Used for titles and headings to capture the futuristic font style that Sony employed for many years.
  • Roboto: Used for body text for clean and readable paragraphs.
